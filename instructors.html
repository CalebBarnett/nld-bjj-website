Head Instructor

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Black Belt

Jesse Hernandez, known as “Tartaruga”, is the Head Instructor at NLD Judo & Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. With over three decades of experience in both judo and Brazilian jiu-jitsu, Coach Jesse brings a deep, fundamentals-first approach rooted in traditional martial arts principles.

Judo Experience

Coach Jesse has practiced judo since 1993 and is currently ranked USA Judo Nidan (2nd Degree Black Belt). He has been a USA Judo Certified Coach since 2012.
